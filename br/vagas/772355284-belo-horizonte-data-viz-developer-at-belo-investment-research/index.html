[Belo Horizonte] Data-Viz Developer @ Belo Investment Research

Descrição da vaga

Role description Develop front-end interface for data analysis applications, as well as dashboards, to visually display quantitative information. Design and format standardized reports and automate front-end processes. While we are a financial research boutique, we’re looking for a data visualization person to support the analytics team. Main activities Development of data-driven documents using HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript. Collaborate with data analysis team to support visualization of quantitative information. Working with data to create views for investment analysts and other business users. Simplify and distill complex processes into intuitive and elegant UIs. Deployment of apps to AWS.

Local

Belo Horizonte - Minas Gerais

Skills: Required: Strong proficiency in JavaScript Knowledge of relational databases.
